# Medical Policy: Knee Arthroscopy with Meniscectomy (CPT 29881)

Effective: 2026-01-01 · Payer: Acme Health Plan · Policy ID: SURG-029

## Prior Authorization Criteria

Knee arthroscopy with medial OR lateral meniscectomy (CPT 29881) is considered
medically necessary when ALL of the following criteria are met:

1. Documented meniscal tear confirmed by MRI or prior arthroscopy.
2. Presence of mechanical symptoms (locking, catching, giving way) OR joint-line
   pain with a positive McMurray test.
3. Failure of at least 6 weeks of conservative therapy, including at least one of:
   physical therapy, NSAIDs, activity modification, or intra-articular injection.
4. Absence of advanced osteoarthritis in the affected compartment
   (Kellgren-Lawrence grade 4).

## Exclusions

- Asymptomatic incidental meniscal tears found on imaging.
- Primary treatment of osteoarthritis pain without discrete tear.

## Documentation Requirements

Clinical notes must include imaging report, conservative therapy timeline, and
examination findings supporting mechanical symptoms.
